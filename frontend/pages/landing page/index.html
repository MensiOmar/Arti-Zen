How we craft at Arti'zen

The story behind Arti'Zen

Crafting

is my therapy.

~Kate J. Richards~

Artizen is a non-profit association dedicated to supporting craftsmen and artisans in selling their unique and high-quality handmade products. The association was founded with the goal of promoting traditional craftsmanship and preserving the art of handmade goods.

We provide a platform for craftsmen to showcase their talents and market their products to a broader audience, enabling them to generate income and sustain their livelihoods. By connecting craftsmen with potential buyers and providing marketing and logistical support, Artizen aims to help these artisans thrive in the modern marketplace.

The association's mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem that benefits both the craftsmen and the community at large, and to foster appreciation for the value and beauty of handmade products.

About Us

Artizen supports craftsmen in selling their handmade products, promotes traditional craftsmanship, and fosters appreciation for the value and beauty of handmade goods.
